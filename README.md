AssemblyPro 🖥️

AssemblyPro is a custom PC building and consultation platform that helps users design their ideal computer system based on their needs and budget.

Unlike traditional e-commerce platforms, AssemblyPro provides a personalized experience where customers configure their preferred PC components, submit their build request, and receive expert consultation before the final order is confirmed.

🚀 Project Overview

Building a custom PC can be challenging due to the wide variety of components available in the market. AssemblyPro simplifies this process by providing an intuitive platform where users can:

Select a PC category
Customize components
Review configurations
Submit build requests
Receive expert consultation
Confirm orders
Get professionally assembled systems
🎯 Problem Statement

Many users struggle with:

Choosing compatible PC components
Understanding hardware specifications
Balancing performance and budget
Finding trustworthy consultation

AssemblyPro addresses these challenges through a guided customization and consultation process.

💡 Solution

AssemblyPro offers a centralized platform where customers can configure their dream PC and receive professional assistance before making a purchase decision.
