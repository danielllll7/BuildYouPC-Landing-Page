FAQ

Frequently asked
questions

What do people need before requesting a build?

Share your main purpose, budget range, preferred look, monitor resolution, and any must-have parts. If you are unsure, Assembly Pro can recommend a balanced starting point.

Can I bring parts I already own?

Yes. Send the exact model names and condition of your existing components. We will check compatibility, power requirements, and whether the parts still make sense for your goals.

Do you support accessories and full setups?

Absolutely. We can pair your PC with monitors, keyboards, mice, headsets, docks, capture gear, and creator accessories that fit the way you work or play.

How long does a custom PC take?

Most builds are assembled, stress-tested, and prepared for delivery within 3 to 7 business days after components are confirmed and available.

Can you support the PC after delivery?

Yes. We provide setup guidance, troubleshooting help, upgrade advice, and support for performance or thermal questions after your system arrives.
